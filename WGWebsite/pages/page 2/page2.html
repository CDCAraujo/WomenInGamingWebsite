DIFDICULTIES WOMEN FACE IN THE GAMING INDUSTRY

In times past, gaming was a novelty reserved for masculine and tech-savvy men, however the online gaming industry is witnessing the number of women players growing steadily on both consumer and developer level. Despite not falling short of their male counter parts, this does not mean female gamers are overtly welcomed with open hands, there are quite a few serious issues every female gamer has dealt with. From sexual harassment to sexism, there is also the issue of gender pay gap and the ignorance of women as a target group of the gaming industry. These and many others pose issues that need to be addressed and communities where women feel safe and welcome are a necessity. Times are changing though, be part of the change and help us spread awareness and fight these issues!
